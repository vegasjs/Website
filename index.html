We plan and manage all of our talks out in the open using GitHub.

If there's a talk you'd like to see or give, add it as an issue here or just shout it out during the planning phase of our next meetup.

Remember: Sometimes the best person to give the talk to you want to hear is you. Researching iving a talk on something is a great way to learn about it. We love first time speakers! (You may even get a t-shirt)
